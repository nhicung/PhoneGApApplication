Useful Tips

The SSF would like to thank Dona Frosio, a Sjögren’s patient and author of the Sjögren’s Self-Help booklet, and our publications committee for once again putting together a wonderful resource.

Dry Eye

97% Sjögren's patients experience dry eye symptoms


Dry Mouth

97% Sjögren's patients experience dry mouth symptoms


Fatigue

94% Sjögren's patients experience fatigue symptoms


Dry Or Itchy Skin

93% Sjögren's patients experience dry or itchy skin symptoms


Morning Stiffness

69% Sjögren's patients experience morning stiffness symptoms almost weekly or frequently


Trouble Sleeping

The vast majority of Sjögren’s patients reported having experienced trouble sleeping (91%).


Joint Pain

Patients over 60 years of age reported that they were more likely than patients 60 years of age and under to experience joint pain (67% vs. 62%) almost weekly or more frequently.


Dry Nose

Patients over 60 years of age reported that they were more likely than patients 60 years of age and under to experience dry nose (65% vs. 59%).


Forgetfulness

The vast majority of Sjögren’s patients reported having experienced forgetfulness (90%) over the last year.


Brain Fog

Patients 60 years of age and under said they were more likely than patients over age 60 to experience brain fog (i.e., confusion,forgetfulness, and lack of focus and mental clarity) (66% vs. 53%).
