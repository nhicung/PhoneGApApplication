The main symptoms are dry mouths and dry eyes.

Patient may experience pain in the joints or muscles.

Patient may experience abnormality of taste, loss of taste, or pins and needles.

Patient may experience dental cavities, dryness, or ulcers.

Patient may experience hoarseness or impaired voice.

Patient may experience difficulty swallowing or dryness.

Patient may also experience other common symptoms such as dry cough, dry eyes, dry skin, fatigue, joint stiffness, stiffness, swollen lymph nodes, or vaginal dryness.
